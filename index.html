Comparative Analysis of Image Editing Models

SwiftEdit, DMD2, and Qwen-Edit Performance Evaluation

Abstract

This study presents a comprehensive comparison of three state-of-the-art image editing models across 15 diverse test cases:

  • Qwen-Edit – Advanced editing model focused on accuracy and detail preservation
  • SwiftEdit – Fast, efficient model optimized for rapid iterations
  • DMD2 – High-quality diffusion model for detailed manipulations

Each test case applies identical prompts across all models to evaluate their capabilities in handling various editing tasks including object transformation, attribute modification, style transfer, and spatial relationship changes.
